Delete Your Account

We understand that you may want to delete your TasKey account at any time. This page explains the simple process to delete your account.

Steps to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to delete your TasKey account:

  1. Open TasKey App: Launch the TasKey application on your device.
  2. Go to Settings Tab: Navigate to the Settings tab within the app.
  3. Select Delete Account: Look for and select the "Delete Account" option in the settings menu.
  4. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation popup will appear. Select "Delete Account" to confirm. Once pressed, your account will be permanently deleted.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about deleting your account or need assistance:

  • Email: admin@taskeyutility.in

Important Notice: Account deletion is a non-reversible process. Once your account is deleted, all data associated with your account will be permanently removed. You are solely responsible for this action and its consequences. TasKey accepts no liability for data loss resulting from account deletion.
